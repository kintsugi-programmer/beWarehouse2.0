1877: Phonograph (Edison) - First record & playback
1887: Gramophone (Berliner) - Flat discs
1906: Vacuum Tube Amplifier (De Forest)
1930s: Magnetic Tape (Germany)
1982: Compact Disc - Digital Audio for Public!
